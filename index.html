01 ABOUT / operator profile

I build systems that have to work the first time, in places where second tries are expensive.

I'm a full-stack engineer with a robotics backbone. At Avalor AI I led the first UAV–UGV teaming project — integrating a Milrem UGV and Holybro UAV into the Nexus control platform, and adding LiDAR SLAM to the air vehicles so they could navigate without GPS.

Before that I deployed warehouse-robotics fleets across Europe for Exotec (Decathlon, Lyreco), wrote a multi-target swarm algorithm for my MSc. thesis at TU Delft, and co-founded Househugger, an AI housing-search platform with a hybrid vector search engine.

Today I'm at Supercrop, building a LangGraph RAG stack for agronomy. I like problems with real physics and real users — and I ship the boring parts too: SQL KPIs, scrapers, Docker, on-call.
